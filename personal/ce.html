What is Circular Economy?

什么是循环经济？

Circular economy, unlike linear economy, reduces material use, redesigns materials to be less resource intensive, and recaptures “waste” as a resource to manufacture new materials and products.

循环经济与线性经济不同，它强调减少材料使用，重新设计材料以减少资源消耗，并将“垃圾”重新作为资源来制造新材料和产品。
